Pan Seared Steak

Ingredients

  • New York Steak

Instructions

  • use paper towels to pat the steaks dry to get a perfect sear and reduce oil splatter.
  • just before cooking steaks, sprinkle both sides liberally with salt and pepper.
  • on medium and brush with oil. Using just 1/2 Tbsp oil reduces splatter.
  • add steaks and sear each side 3-4 minutes until a brown crust has formed then use tongs to turn steaks on their sides and sear edges (1 min per edge).
  • melt in butter with quartered garlic cloves and rosemary sprigs. Tilt pan to spoon garlic butter over steaks and cook to your desired doneness (see chart below).
  • Remove steak and rest 10 minutes before slicing against the grain.

Hamburgers

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Onion
  • Cheese

Instructions

  • Combine the ingredients in a bowl.
  • Make four patties.
  • Grill the patties until the juices run clear and the meat is no longer pink in the center
